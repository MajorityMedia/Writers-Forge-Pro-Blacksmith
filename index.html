Questions?

What if I can't make the live sessions?
Everything is recorded. But this works best if you show up live. The transformation happens in the room.
Do I need to have finished the 5-Day Challenge?
No, but you should understand what you're getting into. The Challenge is a good litmus test for whether Pro is right for you.
What if I'm a beginner?
Pro is not for absolute beginners. You should have written at least one complete draft of something before joining. This is about getting better, not getting started.
What's the time commitment?
Minimum 5 hours per week: 90 min coaching call, 60 min pod, 90 min applying feedback, 60 min community. If you don't have 5 hours/week, you're not ready.
Can I pause my membership?
Yes. Life happens. You can pause for up to 3 months per year. Just let me know.
Will this help me sell a script?
This will help you write a better script. Which increases your odds. But I'm not selling you "the secret to Hollywood." I'm teaching you craft and giving you community.
